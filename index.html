About ICRTETM 2K23

Stella Mary's College of Engineering, accredited by NAAC and NBA (MECH & CSE), is proud to organize the International Conference on "Recent Trends in Engineering, Technology and Management" (ICRTETM 2K23). This conference will be conducted in a hybrid mode and aims to bring together researchers, engineers, and academicians to share their innovations and developments.

Important Highlights

  • All accepted papers will be published in Scopus-indexed journals.
  • Submit your paper using the provided template.
  • Explore recent trends in engineering, technology, and management.
