About ICRTETM 2K23

About the Institution

Stella Mary's College of Engineering, promoted by the Nova Educational Trust, was established by the visionary business entrepreneur, Dr. Nazareth Charles. The college is dedicated to empowering the underprivileged and creating skillful professionals for the industry and the nation.

Accredited by NAAC and NBA, Stella Mary's offers cutting-edge infrastructure, a nurturing academic environment, and exceptional placement opportunities, including international placements. The institution is renowned for fostering innovation, research, and entrepreneurial skills, setting global standards in education.

About the Department of Mechanical Engineering

Since its inception in 2013, the Department of Mechanical Engineering has consistently delivered academic excellence, supported by state-of-the-art infrastructure and a highly qualified faculty. With a focus on research, innovation, and professional development, the department emphasizes industry-relevant education through workshops, industrial visits, and hands-on project opportunities.

The department is dedicated to equipping students with the skills to face industry challenges and excel in professional careers in India and abroad. The Design and Fabrication Laboratory fosters innovative projects, enabling students to explore cutting-edge technologies.

Vision

To impart nationally and internationally recognized education in Mechanical Engineering, shaping innovative engineers who excel in the profession and research.

Mission

  • Provide a world-class education that equips students with the ability to design, plan, and manage modern mechanical engineering technologies.
  • Prepare students to face future challenges in industries and society.
  • Enable students to secure leading positions in industry, academia, and PSUs in India and abroad.
